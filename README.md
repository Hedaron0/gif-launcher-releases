# GIF Launcher

**Share moments in real time. GIFs, clips, memes — synced across everyone's screen.**

GIF Launcher lets you play videos, GIFs, and audio in perfect sync across multiple devices over a shared channel. Built for friends who want to react together, share the moment, or just vibe — no screen sharing, no lag, no hassle.

---

## What it does

- 🎬 **Real-time media sync** — play a GIF or video and everyone in your channel sees it at the same time
- 🔗 **URL-based** — paste any GIF, video, or YouTube link and launch it instantly
- 🎵 **Audio support** — drop sound effects or music clips for the whole group
- ⚡ **Zero delay** — WebSocket-powered sync keeps everyone in frame
- 🖥️ **Borderless overlay** — media plays on top of whatever you're doing, no interruptions
- 🎮 **Built for gamers** — perfect for in-game moments, watch parties, and group reactions

---

## Perfect for

- Reacting to a clip mid-game without alt-tabbing
- Dropping a meme at exactly the right moment
- Creating shared memories with friends online
- Watch parties, sync'd highlights, group humor
